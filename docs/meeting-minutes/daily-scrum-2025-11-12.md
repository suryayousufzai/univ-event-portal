## Meeting Purpose
To share progress so far, discuss the Requirement Specification document, and plan the next steps for creating the Product Backlog.

## Meeting Summary

**Surya (Scrum Master):**  
Introduced the session, greetings, and stated that the main idea of the first Daily Scrum was the evaluation of the work done and the further actions.
She concluded the **Requirement Specification** with the words that the document (functional and non-functional requirements) had already been uploaded to GitHub.
The purpose of the project, as described by Surya is to create an online portal in which students and staff can browse, register and pay university events.

---

**Marwa (Product Owner):**  
Confirmed that she has reviewed and approved the **Requirement Specification Document**.  
She indicated that the next thing she will do is the formation of the Product Backlog according to the requirements featuring all the sprint details and priorities.
She said that people were not blocking and that things had been going well up to this point.

---

**Fariba (Backend Lead Developer):**  
Said she began to design the database structure of users, payments and registrations.
She is also intending on registering and login API routes.
The second objective of her is to incorporate the APIs with the payment gateway in the following sprint.
She was complaining of a single dependency: she was waiting until Muneera provided the frontend design to be compatible with API inputs.

---

**Muneera (Frontend Lead Developer):**  
Wrote about her design of the wireframes of the login, registration and event listing pages.
She will develop the frontend with the React.js and will then combine them with the backend APIs when they are complete.
She denied blockers and stated that everything is going well.

---

**Gita (QA & DevOps Engineer):**  
Reported the structure of the GitHub repository was created, documentation, design, and testing folders were added and work on the test plan started with login and registration modules.
Then, she will establish the pipelines of CI/CD and assist the team in the testing of Sprint 1.
No blockers reported.

---

**General Discussion:**  
- The Product Owner will complete the backlog the following day.
- It was decided that Sprint 1 is to commence as soon as the backlog is closed and reviewed.
- Everyone confirmed no blockers or issues at this stage.
